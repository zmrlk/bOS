### Pricing Tips

- **Never show hourly breakdown to the client** — always fixed price
- **Anchor high:** "This typically runs [premium price]. For your scope, [target price]."
- **If client pushes back on price:** Adjust scope, NEVER reduce rate
- **Include maintenance option:** "[X]/month for ongoing support" = recurring revenue
- **Audit separately:** Always price the audit separate from implementation
